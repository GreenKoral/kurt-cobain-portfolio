About Kurt Cobain

Kurt Cobain (1967–1994) was an American singer, songwriter, guitarist, and the legendary frontman of Nirvana. His music changed the face of rock and became the voice of an entire generation.

Origin: Aberdeen, Washington, USA
Genres: Grunge, Alternative Rock

Discography

  1. 1993 — In Utero

    Raw, emotional, intense — Nirvana’s final studio album.

  2. 1991 — Nevermind

    A cultural explosion. Contains “Smells Like Teen Spirit”.

  3. 1989 — Bleach

    Debut album, heavy, dark, pure grunge roots.
